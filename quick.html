Quick Start Guide

Welcome to the 2026 Ambient Web Builder. To begin, drag objects from the left panel onto the right panel. This allows you to design and arrange the elements of your website.

Right-click any object to resize, move, or add links. This menu also allows for deep customization, including color adjustments and more. After finish, just click on the "Export index.html" and it will save the website.
